About Jeremy

Jeremy hails from North Haven, Connecticut. He enjoys mapping wild terrain, studying kendo, and embarking on adventures.

When he is not sword fighting he is street fighting. When he is street fighting his code is compiling. If his code is not compiling well I guess he's doing his job...

But since Jeremy loves swordfighting so much, you better be "en garde" at the wedding!

About Marina

Marina grew up in the realm of Columbus, Ohio, but recalls her first memories in the far country of Uzbekistan.

Marina works on top-secret scientific research in preparation for the day when she, as a MAD scientist (with her freeze ray) will rule the world...

But for now she will have to make due with ruling the dance floor at the wedding!

Our Story

It happened one fateful summer day. Jeremy was working at his summer internship while Marina arrived in full excitement for graduate school. The rest took off on its own...6 months later.
After three years of fencing, dancing, and traversing through the landscapes of Middle-Earth on adventures with Hobbits, Dwarves, Elves, and Men, with Mortal and Immortal Folk, we found that we had become inseparable.
